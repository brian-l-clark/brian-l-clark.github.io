Retention and Churn

Reducing Churn Through Behavioral Segmentation

At FullSpeed Automotive, broad batch-and-blast campaigns were missing customers at the moments that mattered. I rebuilt the segmentation model around behavioral signals: visit cadence, service history, and engagement drop-off patterns. Targeted journeys re-engaged at-risk customers before they lapsed. AI-assisted copy production kept iteration fast without adding headcount.

Campaign Performance

Scaling High-Volume Email and SMS to 45% Open Rates

With 40+ campaigns per year across a multi-million record database, deliverability, relevance, and timing each had to be engineered, not guessed at. A structured A/B testing program for subject lines, send times, and content formats doubled click-through rates and maintained a 45% average open rate at scale. Every insight fed back into the next brief.
